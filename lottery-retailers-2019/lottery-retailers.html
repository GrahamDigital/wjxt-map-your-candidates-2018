This map shows the collective value of winning tickets sold by lottery retailer, as well as how many draw game tickets and scratch-off tickets were sold there, during 2017 and 2018.

Use the menu on the right side of the map to switch between prize totals, draw game sales, and scratch-off sales.
Circles with numbers mean there is a cluster of retailers in one area - click on the dot to zoom in.
Green pins indicate smaller prize totals or ticket sales, red pins indicate higher prize totals or ticket sales, and orange pins indicate in-between amounts. Click on a pin to see a retailer's exact totals.
